You can scan exam papers using your smartphones easily. If you have a scanner machine, you can use that without giving it a second thought since they're much faster. This guide is for people who want to scan the papers using their smartphones.

If you've conducted your exam successfully, there's one step left: transferring the papers to Paper Grading. Paper Grading can grade your papers with nearly 100% accuracy. For this, you need to transfer physical exam papers to digital as highest quality as possible. To achieve this, please consider these general rules below:

  • Scan papers in the brightest part of the room. This is very important. If possible, scan them directly under a light or sunlight.
  • Don't shake phone camera while scanning. Shaken photo means no information can be extracted from that image. Don't forget that if you don't have enough light when you scan, your camera can shake without you noticing.
  • Scan the papers in order, this is also extremely important. If your exam has more than one side, don't mix the order of students. It should go like this: "Student1-First Side.jpg, Student1-Second Side.jpg" and so on. Don't forget that Paper Grading grades papers in order.

This guide is divided into two categories: Android and iOS smartphones. Ease of use order goes like this:

For Android:

  • Samsung Galaxy camera app (Usage: Easy)
  • Genius Scan for Android (Usage: Very Easy, Recommended)

For iOS:

  • Taking photo (Usage: Easy)
  • Genius Scan for iOS (Usage: Very Easy, Recommended)

Scanning With Android Phones

Using Samsung Galaxy Camera

In some versions of Samsung Galaxy smartphones, there's a scanning app integrated to its camera. If you preper to use it, you can scan documents easily without downloading Genius Scan. Let's check if your smartphone has this feature.

Open camera app on your smartphone, then click on settings symbol on the top left of your screen. Click on "Scene Optimizer" text, not on the switch button. If you don't see scene optimizer on your settings, please proceed with Genius Scan alternative. On the new screen, activate "Document Scanner" option.
Warning: if there's no such option, you need to use Genius Scan, we'll tell you how under the next title.

Have you activated document scanner? Great. Now let's use this powerful document scanner of Samsung Galaxy.


First, put the paper on a table with a dark color and start scanning the documents. It's just enough to put your camera on the document without clicking any button, you'll see a button on which "Click to scan" is written on a yellow background. Click that to scan the document and fix the scanned parts if necessary.

If everything's alright, click "Save" button on the right bottom of your screen. That's all! You need to repeat this for every document you have.

After scanning every document you have, upload all of them to Paper Grading starting with this step:


On Paper Grading grade screen upload all the scanned documents via browsing your smartphone's files.

Using Genius Scan

GeniusScan is a free app that can scan documents in high qualities.If your smartphone doesn't support feature of integrated document scanner or if you want an easier solution, you can prefer using this.

After downloading GeniusScan, just for once, you need to change a few settings.


Open GeniusScan and click "Menu" button on bottom right of your screen. Then click "General" button which is on the top. Burada yapmamız gereken önemli değişiklikler var. First click "Image Quality" and choose "Highest", after that choose "None" in "Default Filters" setting.

Reminder:You don't need to go through these settings again, you can just start scanning next time, they will be saved as default preferences.

After you've completed this part, go back to the main menu and click folder symbol at the bottom right of you screen to start scanning.


Genius Scan can scan the documents without you clicking anything else. You'll see a border will be created while you put the document directly under the camera. After you've done scanning all the documents, click tick (✓) symbol on the bottom right.

You can see every document you've scanned on this screen. Click the field on the top to name your PDF file that is about to be exported. For example: "Class 6A Exams". Then click share symbol on the right top of your screen.


Click "Device Memory" button. After that choose a random folder. For example: Documents.


Then open Paper Grading Grade screen and upload your papers choosing the PDF file you had created. You might see your PDF file as the most recent file on the top.

Scanning With iOS Smartphones

Using "Live" Feature

If your iOS smartphone supports "Live" photo feature, you can scan papers following certain easy rules.

However, this can be more difficult compared to using Genius Scan, thus we still suggest considering Genius Scan option as well.


If you'll use your camera with Live feature, please take photo in a upright angle. As long as not too much, it's not a problem taking background in the image. Most important thing here is not to take photo in skewed angles.

After scanning all the papers, navigate to Paper Grading Grade screen and upload the papers easily.

Using Genius Scan

If you find using your smartphone's camera difficult, use the easiest alternative: GeniusScan . GeniusScan is free and can scan your documents in high quality. Also it's easier to use since it compiles all the documents in a single PDF file.

But before we start, just for once we need to configure Genius Scan for the best use.


Open Genius Scan after downloading it and click the settings symbol on the top right of your screen. Then click "General" setting which is the first option. Change default filter to "None" and go back to the main menu.

Reminder: You don't need to go through these settings again, you can just start scanning next time, they will be saved as default preferences.

On the main menu you'll see a large + button, click that to start scanning.


Hover your camera on the documents, you don't need to click a button to scan them, Genius Scan will identify the documents automatically.

After scanning all the documents, click the button on the bottom right corner. If everything seems okay, click export button on the bottom right corner.


On exporting screen click Files option. Then name the PDF file that is about to be created. For example: "Class 6A"


After you name the document, choose a random folder to save in. For instance: Downloads folder. Then we're ready. Navigate to Paper Grading Grade screen and upload the papers from the folder you had chosen to save. That's all!
