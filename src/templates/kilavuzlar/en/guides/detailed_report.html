If you're reading this guide right now, it means that you've completed these steps successfully:

  • Creating An Exam
  • Scanning The Exam Papers
  • Entering An Answer Key

Congratualions! If you share your experiences so far with us via e-mail, we'd be so happy.

If you've successfully created an answer key and verified the exam, Paper Grading will redirect you to the main page and will give "Your request has been queued" notification to you. After your exams have been graded, you'll get another notification informing you that the process has finished.

Click on the notication or navigate to Panel > Graded Exams to see your graded exams.

Click on the exam you want to view.

You'll find more details about the exam you had clicked on. Results List tab shows all students' results in a list. If you associated some learning objectives with questions on that exam, Learning Objectives Analyses will show the students' performance with the associated learning objectives.

To change the title of the exam, click on the default title. You can write a new title of maximum 100 characters.

Associating The Exam With A Class

If you created a Class, you can link this exam with that class to track students' performance for a year. Click on "Link with a class" button to do that.


On the pop up you'll see classes you've created. In that list, choose the class you want to link the exam with and hit Save button.

Paper Grading will match the students automatically. It can make a mistake rarely, in those cases click on the Edit symbol and link that exam paper to the right student.

Viewing Detailed Reports Of Students

To view the detailed report of a student, click on the row of that student. This will redirect you to detailed report screen.

If the exam was associated with learning objectives, you can view student's performance clicking on Learning Objectives Analyses tab.

When Paper Grading is not confident enough about a decision it's made, it will highlight that queston. You need to be more careful about these questions. If you detected a wrong decision by Paper Grading, click on the result on Student Answers column and fix that result.

Exporting The Student Report

You can click on Export Report tab to export student's report as a PDF file.

Don't forget, it's your responsibility to check all the results before announcing them.
